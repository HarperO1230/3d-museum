Rock-O-Bots

The Rock-O-Bots are a factory-based band consisting of Blocko, the guitarist, and Pixel, the lead singer. Their world tour for their new album, Decoding Bugs, begins in January of 2026, with stops from Korea to Hawaii.

Their music blends robotic precision with glitchy creativity, turning machine noise into melody and emotion. “Decoding Bugs” is their first full-length album, exploring themes of circuitry, error, and electric dreams.
